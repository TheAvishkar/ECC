Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the Engineers Connect Club?

+

To join ECC, you can attend our monthly meetings, fill out a membership form, or contact us directly. We welcome all students interested in electronics and communication engineering.

Are there any membership fees?

+

No, there was nothing membership fees .

What kind of events do you organize?

+

We organize technical workshops, guest lectures, industry visits, project competitions, hackathons, and our annual tech symposium. Check our Events page for upcoming activities.

Can any department students join the club?

+

While our focus is on skill development, we welcome students from all engineering disciplines who have an interest in our field.
